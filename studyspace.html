Who are we ?

Study Space is composed of a community of learners; from students, teachers, working professions, and even the average book worm. If you're motivated to learn something new or are studying something for the first time, this is the place for you!

What it's About?

Study Space is a simple to use web app that connects like-minded and eager individuals who are keen on collaboration and Teamwork! This is a site for those who believe working together and that two, three or even four minds are better than one. There's no limit with your study mates!
